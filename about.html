About

Hello! I’m Silvia,
a 22-year-old designer based in Rome, Italy.

I'm a recent Design graduate from La Sapienza University in Rome,
with a focus on graphic, product, and exhibition design.

I'm passionate about UX/UI design, typography, and editorial work ,
and I also have a strong interest in urban and interior design.

My approach is human-centered, and throughout my academic journey I've explored a wide range of disciplines, from branding and interface design to print and spatial projects.
